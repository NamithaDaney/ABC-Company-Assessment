# ABC-Company-Assessment
Preprocessing data, analysis and find insights.

Working with a dataset from ABC company,consisting of 458 rows and 9 columns. The company requires a comprehensive report detailing information about their employees across various teams. Which includes preprocessing the dataset, analyzing the data, and presenting the findings graphically. 

Corrected the data in the "height" column by replacing it with random numbers between 150 and 180. Handled null values of Columns College (used Mode)and Salary(used Mean).

Determined the distribution of employees across each team and calculate the percentage split relative to the total number of employees. The distribution of employees across each team almost similar. Segregated the employees based on their positions within the company and identified that the position 'SG' has highest number of employees(110). The position 'C' has comparatively lower number of employees(80). Then identified the predominant age group among employees that is employees aged 24 are dominant in the company. The age group of 20-30 is highest among all other age groups. The analysis discovered that the team-position combination with the highest salary expenditure is 'Los Angeles Lakers' in the 'SF' position, with a total salary expenditure of 31,866,445.0. On the other hand, 'Orlando Magic' in the 'SF' position has the minimum salary expenditure of 845,059.0. In addition, we discovered that there is no specific correlation between age and salary. Employees across all age groups appear to have almost similar minimum salary levels.

For each of the analysis tasks mentioned above, appropriate visualizations were created to present the findings effectively.
